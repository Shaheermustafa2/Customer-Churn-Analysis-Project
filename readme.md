## Customer Churn Analysis Project



#### Overview



This project analyzes customer churn behavior in a telecom company to identify key factors influencing customer retention.



#### Objectives


* Identify customers most likely to churn
* Analyze impact of contract type, tenure, and pricing
* Provide business recommendations to reduce churn


Tools \& Technologies
---


* Python (pandas, NumPy)
* Data Visualization (Matplotlib, Seaborn)
* Dashboard: Power BI


Project Workflow
---


* Data Cleaning and Preprocessing
* Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)
* Feature-based churn analysis
* Dashboard creation in Power BI
* Business insights and recommendations


Key Insights
---


* Month-to-month contract customers have highest churn
* Customers with tenure < 1 year are more likely to leave
* High monthly charges increase churn probability
* Electronic check users show higher churn behavior


Business Recommendations
---


* Encourage long-term contracts with discounts
* Improve onboarding experience for new customers
* Promote automatic payment methods
* Optimize pricing strategy for high-charge customers
